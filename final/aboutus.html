Our Story

In 1956 our grandparents, Rudy and Emily, met and fell in love at the Pacific Coast. Rudy and Emily eventually married and after saving all their earnings, built the Pacific Coast Resort to share their love of nature with others.

The Pacific Coast Resort offers many fun activities, allowing guests from all walks of life to unplug from their busy lives, relax, and get in tune with mother earth. Whether you are traveling alone, with a partner, or in a group, we activities for all.
